Dar Ul Buruz Quran Khatam

When people gather to recite the Qur’an, tranquility descends upon them, mercy envelops them, and the angels surround them.
The Prophet ﷺ was asked: “What is the way out on that Day, O Messenger of Allah?”
He replied: “The Book of Allah and my Sunnah.”
If we do not read today, a day will come when we will wish that we had.
Take part — and seek the pleasure of the One who created you, by returning to His Book.
